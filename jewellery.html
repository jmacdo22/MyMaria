Jewellery


Looking for a beautiful handcrafted gift, or maybe something for yourself?

Maria makes beautiful natural jewellery.

Most of her peices are made of wiigwaas or birch bark. Wiigwaas is the Ojibwe term for birch bark.

Maria has also made some earrings using animal hydes. She is a wonderfully talented artist and her earrings do not stay in stock for long. Since every peice is handcrafted they are in limited supply.

Maria has also been known to donate earrings to good causes such as raffling them off to raise money for a friend recently diagnosed with cancer.
