About







Meet the woman behind it all!

Maria is a young metis woman living in the Rainy River District.

She is active in her community serving on town council, working at the local mine, and volunteering her time to local causes.

Maria spends time at the Stratton Salesbarn, working, baking, crafting, creating and farming.
